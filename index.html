Welcome.

Hello! Thank you for taking the time to view my portfolio. I am pleased that you are interested in learning a bit about me, and happy to oblige.

As of now I am just beginning as a web developer, having completed a three month full-stack web development bootcamp at the University of Denver. Although this was a fairly short program it was very time and labor intensive and I can confidently say I am ready for a junior position as a web developer - I have more to learn (hopefully always will), but I am a skilled student and researcher and ready to continue on the job. So, I am looking for a company that is willing to take me on in return for my sincere efforts to grow as an asset to their team. As a part of a team I offer creativity and a dynamic perspective, an openness to influencing and being influenced by others, persistence, and reliable equanimity. In the meantime, I am exploring opportunities to volounteer code, and collaborating on projects with my friends from the DU program. Initially I was attracted to software development and information technology because it is vibrantly alive and the applications seem diverse and endless, as do the career opportunities. I have discovered that I am indirectly passionate about code because curiosity is a fundamental part of my being, a core value, and you could say that this particular art and science lends itself very adequately to the purpose of increasing ones awareness of the world. As well as assisting others with acquiring greater awareness for themselves. The field of data visualization is particularly interesting to me and where I am currently focusing my personal efforts.

Please enjoy my portfolio. And please remember to take in to account that although new to this particular field of work, I have a decade of good work behind me in my previous career and am no stranger to adversity, challenge, the nature of perseverance, or success. While I am eager, I am also interested in a good fit. I think patience and persistence yield the best results the fastest on average.
